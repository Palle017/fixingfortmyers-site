Trucks, Diesels, And Long Commutes

Diesel Trucks & No-Starts

Lehigh Acres runs on trucks — work trucks, tow rigs, diesel pickups that earn their keep. When one cranks slow, hard-starts on a humid morning, or flat refuses to fire, that's not an inconvenience, that's a lost workday. Diesel repair and no-start diagnosis are core work at this shop: fuel delivery, glow plug circuits, batteries and cables, cranking systems — tested in a proper bay, not guessed at in a parking lot.

Front Ends That Take A Beating

Anyone who drives Lehigh's grid streets every day knows what they do to a front end. Clunks over bumps, wandering steering, uneven tire wear — that's suspension and steering work, and catching it early is a lot cheaper than replacing tires every year and hoping.

Commuter Wear

If you commute from Lehigh into Fort Myers daily, your car eats miles most people don't put on in a week. Stop-and-go traffic chews through brakes, Florida heat works the AC year-round, and high mileage catches up with the transmission. Warning light on and no clear answer? Start with diagnostics so the repair matches the actual fault.

The Distance Killer

Can't Spare The Trip? Concierge Pickup & Return

Working a full day, one vehicle in the household, or just no time to sit around a shop? Concierge pickup and return is a paid add-on that solves the distance question completely: Tony picks your vehicle up in Lehigh Acres, does the work at the shop with real equipment, and brings it back when it's done. This is not mobile mechanic work in your driveway — the repair happens in the bay where it should — but the driving gets handled for you.

You keep your day. The truck gets fixed. The only thing you skip is the drive.
