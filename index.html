What We Do

Our Services

From a dead battery to a full engine rebuild — if it's broken, noisy, leaking, shaking, or lighting up your dash, we're on it.

Diagnostics

State-of-the-art diagnostic tools and equipment. Check engine, ABS, airbag, transmission codes — we have the technology to find it fast and fix it right.

A/C Service

Certified A/C Repair. We keep you cooler than a penguin in sunglasses. Recharge, leak repair, compressor replacement, evaporator service, R-134a and R-1234yf systems.

Brakes

Pads, rotors, calipers, brake lines, master cylinder, ABS module repairs. We stop problems before they stop you.

Engine Repair

Timing chains, head gaskets, mounts, seals, valve cover gaskets, full engine swaps. Built different, built to last.

Transmission

Service, fluid flush, solenoids, valve body, full rebuilds, and swaps including junkyard units with proper programming and adaptive resets.

Module Programming

OEM-level ECM, TCM, BCM, and TPMS programming. VIN writes, software flashes, and key programming. We do what most shops can't.

Cooling System

Radiator, thermostat, water pump, hoses, coolant flush. Florida heat is no joke — keep your engine cool all year.

Suspension & Steering

Control arms, ball joints, tie rods, struts, shocks, power steering. If it's handling wrong, we straighten it out.

Battery & Electrical

Battery replacement, alternator, starter, wiring diagnostics. Dead battery, bad alternator, starter issues — call Tony and we'll get you sorted fast.

Oil Change & Maintenance

Conventional, synthetic, high-mileage. Filter, fluid top-offs, inspection. Book your appointment and bring it in.

Exhaust & Emissions

Catalytic converter, O2 sensors, muffler, flex pipe, DPF cleaning. Keep it clean, keep it legal.

No-Start Diagnosis

No-crank, crank-no-start, intermittent starting, parasitic draw, weak batteries, starter faults, relay issues, and charging-system problems. We diagnose the cause instead of guessing.

Diesel Service

Diesel diagnostics, fuel systems, injectors, turbos, DEF/DPF service, and repairs. Gas or diesel — Tony works on it all.

Car Audio & Stereo

Full car audio blueprint and build. Head units, amps, subs, speakers, custom wiring — we design the system and install it right.

Race Mods & Performance

Forced induction, engine builds, suspension tuning, exhaust upgrades. Built to go fast. Built to last. No limits.

Old School Hot Rods

Classic builds, restorations, and custom fabrication. Old school iron with new school performance. This is the stuff Tony lives for.

Custom Upgrades

Wheels, tires, lighting, body work, custom fabrication — if you can dream it, we can build it. Your vehicle, your vision.

Premium Add-On

Concierge Service

We pick up your vehicle, bring it to our shop, fix it right, and return it to your door. Additional fee applies. Just call Tony to arrange.
